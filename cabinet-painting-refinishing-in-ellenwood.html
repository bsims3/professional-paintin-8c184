What is your process for painting or refinishing cabinets?
We start by removing cabinet doors and hardware, then thoroughly clean and sand all surfaces to ensure great paint adhesion. After that, we apply a quality primer and multiple coats of durable paint or stain, using spray equipment for a smooth, factory-like finish. Finally, we reinstall doors and hardware, making sure everything is clean and functioning well before we leave.
